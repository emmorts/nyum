Manna porridge★ Manų košė

  • 2 servings
  • 20 minutes
  • Sonata Stropuvienė

This creamy manna porridge is usually consumed as a nutritious breakfast, a light snack, or a simple dessert. Usually enjoyed warm, the bowl of this warm porridge is lightly sweetened and traditionally served drizzled with butter. It is recommended to garnish it with fresh fruit or various preserves.

  • 500 ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 20 ml water

Heat milk on medium-high heat. Add a bit of water, sugar, salt and stir until it starts boiling, about 8 to 10 minutes.

  • 3 tbsp manna

Very slowly start adding manna and stirring, to avoid having balls of manna. Once all manna has been stirred in, continue stirring until the mass thickens, for about 5 to 10 minutes.

Once the desired consistency is reached, serve the manna porridge with desired toppings.

Enjoy!
